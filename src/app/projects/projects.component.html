Ghost Writers

Ghost Writers uses Wikitude's image recognition software to create a mobile augmented reality graffiti application that allows users to post and create their own virtual art, anywhere in the world.

Triplit

Triplit is a web application that makes it easy for you and your friends to plan trips in as easy as three steps. Create a trip, invite your friends, find a hotel, and depart on your adventure.
